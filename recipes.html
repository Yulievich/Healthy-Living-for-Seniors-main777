Salmon with Roasted Vegetables

This delicious and healthy meal is perfect for seniors who want to maintain a nutritious diet. The salmon provides omega-3 fatty acids, which can help reduce inflammation, and the roasted vegetables are packed with vitamins and minerals.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb. salmon fillet
  • 2 cups chopped vegetables (such as broccoli, bell peppers, and onions)
  • 2 tbsp. olive oil
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • 1/2 tsp. black pepper

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 400Â°F.
  2. Toss vegetables with olive oil, salt, and pepper. Spread on a baking sheet and roast for 20 minutes.
  3. Add salmon to the baking sheet and roast for an additional 10-15 minutes, until salmon is cooked through.
